About the Board

Protecting California consumers by promoting ethical standards and enforcing health and safety regulations since 1927.

Our Mission

The Board of Barbering and Cosmetology protects consumers by licensing and regulating the individuals and establishments that provide barbering, cosmetology, and electrology services in the State of California.

We are committed to transparency, excellence in service, and the proactive enforcement of health and safety standards across all regulated professions.
